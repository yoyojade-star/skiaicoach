\# SkiAI Coaching Skills



\## 1. Carving Analysis

\- \*\*Target Joint Angles:\*\* Knee flexion 110°-130°, Hip angulation > 25°.

\- \*\*Key Metric:\*\* Carving Score (0-100) based on edge pressure and stability.



\## 2. Fault Identification

\- \*\*Backseat:\*\* Detected when CoM z-index is < 40% of the distance between heel and toe.

\- \*\*A-Frame:\*\* Detected when the inner knee angle of the outside leg varies > 5° from the inside leg.



\## 3. Recommended Drills

\- \*\*The Thousand Steps:\*\* For weight transfer and edge finding.

\- \*\*Sword Fight:\*\* For upper-body stability and hand positioning.
